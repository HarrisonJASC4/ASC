A TimeLine of Kanye West's Muscical Career:

From young kid growing up in the streets of SoHo Chicago to A-List Controversal Superstar Globally Known

Born June 8th 1977, in Atlanta. Kanye Omari West was raised in Chicago.

1990: Kanye's mother, Donda West pays Kanye to cut his first record in his basement

August 1996: At the age of 19 Kanye delivers an aa freestyle at Fats Beats storefront in Greenwich Village. This freestly has now become a great time-capsule for the future generations

1998: Kanye sells his first beat for $8,000 to local Chicago rapper Gravity. His next beat for Jermaine Dupri's debut album for $5,000. Months later was found featuring on Ma$e's Harlem World group album. After all this he opted to drop out of Chicago State University to purse his music dreams.Offered a record contract by Columbia Records but things fell apart.

1988-99: Kanye teamed up with Timmy G, Arrowstar, and GLC to form the group called the "Go Getters".

2000: This was the year where Kanye's music career started to take off. Ye, started producing for Roc-A-Fella Records artists, Beanie Sigel and Jay-Z.

2001: Kanye's name starts to gain more recognition due to his work on Jay-Z's album the "Blueprint". Kanye's help on such hit songs such as"Izzo (H.O.V.A.)" and "Takeover" which became critical and commercial success for Jay-Z and can be debated as his greatest works, led to the demand of more Kanye West.

2002: Kanye West is terribly injured in a near-fatal car crash, after falling asleep behind the wheel. Forced to have surgery, Kanye's jaw is wired shut, to most people they thought this meant the end of Kanye's career. But Kanye instead used this as motivation to record his debut single "Through the Wire".

2003-4: Kanye drops his debut album "The College Dropout". On this album, Kanye also recored his first chart topping single in "Slow Jamz" which made it to No.1 in Hot 100, Rap, and R&B charts."he College Dropout" released in 04, sold 441,000 copies the first week and debuted at No.2 on the Billboard 200 albums chart.Dubbed triple platnium and an overall success to both critics and fans. Riding the success of his first album Kanye fouded his own record label GOOD Music. Kanye also finds John Legend and helps bring him in the spotlight by support Legend's debut signe, "Used to Love You"

2005: Kanye wins Garmmy Awards for Best Rap Ablum("The College Dropout") and Best Rap song("Jesus Walks"). TIME's 100 Most Influential People. Also in this year, Kanye says one his most controversial things yet. Following Hurricane Katrina on live tv Kanye says "George Bush doesn't care about Black People"

2007: Kanye releases his 3rd album "Graduation" which in its opening week sold 957,000 units. This album is also credited for ending the era of gangster music, when it curshed 50 Cent's album "Curtis". Later this year in November, Donda West passes away.
